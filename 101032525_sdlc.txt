	Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is a procedure used by the software industry to create, evolve and evaluate high quality softwares. SDLC is a process followed for a software project, within a software organization. It is made up of a precise blueprint characterizing how to cultivate, keep, supplant and adjust or augment specific software. The life cycle gives a description of a methodology for bolstering the quality of software and the overall development process.

	The SDLC is important to a DevOps engineer because it helps them design a high-quality software that matches or goes beyond the customers expectations, comes to fruition and completion within the appropriate time and finnancial estimates. Peering at the software development lifecycle through the scope of a DevOps engineer will create a more knowledgeable and adequate developer.
