Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is a procedure used by the software industry to create, evolve and evaluate high quality softwares. The SDLC is important to a DevOps engineer because it helps them design a high-quality software that matches or goes beyond the customers expectations, comes to a conclusion within the appropriate time and finnancial estimates. Peering at the software development lifecycle through the scope of a DevOps Engineer will create a more knowledgeable and adequate developer.
