COMP 3104 DevOps course is made to equip professionals approaching the IT field. Specifically, an area where servers, web applications and infrastructure will be absolute key to business needs. COMP 3104 will offer a hands-on experience through the implementation of tried, tested and true DevOps technologies. With understanding how to utilize Amazon Web Services (AWS), Ansible, Docker and Travis CI, you will become knowledgeable with end-to-end deployment processes.
