DevOps
---------------------
DevOps COMP 3104 is designed to prepare students to handle servers, 
web apps and infrastracture through hands-on experience with DevOps
technology. In this course will showcase the importance of DevOps
and how companies applied these strategies. Using DevOps effectively
will allow students to become better developer through working with
a team and seeing how the DevOps strategies allows for easier way
to ship codes and handle bugs.
