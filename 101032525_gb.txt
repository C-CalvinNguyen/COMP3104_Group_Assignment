	George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is partially supported by the government financially, completely accredited college of applied arts and technology with three large learning grounds in the heart of Toronto, Canada. George Brown College had its rights and privileges established by means of a charter in 1966 by the Ontario government and operated the following year. 

	Those interested may find programs within the following centres: Arts, Design & information, Business, Community Services & Early Childhood, Construction & Engineering Technologie, Health Sciences, Hospitality & Culinary Arts, Prepatory & Liberal Studies.
