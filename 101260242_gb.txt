[About George Brown college]
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
George Brown College is a public, fully accredited college with three campuses in downtown Toronto -- St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus and Waterfront Campus. 
They provide a blend of theory and hands-on experience. GBC prioritizes real-world learning and provides students with opportunities to work alongside industry 
professionals in applied research and field education opportunities. They offer a wide variety of programs in art and design, business, community services, 
early childhood education, construction and engineering technologies, health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, preparatory studies, and specialized 
programs and services for recent immigrants and international students. Their location itself gives students an edge in the job market. With their industry 
partnerships, students can get the latest trends and tech they expect in the field.
